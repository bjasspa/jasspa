QUESTION (16)

    Timestamp; Format incorrect, how can I change to MMDDYY.hhmm ?

ANSWER

    From within your <user.emf> set the time stamp default format i.e. 

      set-variable $timestamp "<%M%D%Y.%h%m>"

    See $timestamp(5).
