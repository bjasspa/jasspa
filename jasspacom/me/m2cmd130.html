NAME

    suspend-emacs - Suspend editor and place in background

SYNOPSIS

    suspend-emacs

PLATFORM

    Supported on UNIX platforms - irix, hpux, sunos, freebsd or linux.

DESCRIPTION

    suspend-emacs suspends the editing processor and puts it into the background. The "fg" command restarts MicroEmacs '02.
